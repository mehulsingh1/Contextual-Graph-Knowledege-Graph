Project Lumina is a next-generation GraphRAG framework developed by the AI Innovation Lab. 
Dr. Aris Thorne is the Principal Architect of Project Lumina. 
The AI Innovation Lab is a strategic department within Nexus Corp. 
Project Lumina utilizes the Groq Inference Engine to achieve ultra-low latency triplet extraction. 
The Groq Inference Engine runs on LPU (Language Processing Unit) hardware. 
Nexus Corp signed a partnership with Neo4j to provide the graph database backbone for Project Lumina. 
Sarah Chen is the Lead Data Scientist at Neo4j who manages the Nexus Corp integration. 
Project Lumina competes with traditional Vector RAG systems by providing multi-hop reasoning capabilities.
